About GeoFeature

GeoFeature is a website for exploring the geology and landscape of South West England. Focusing on Dorset and the surrounding regions.

GeoFeature is a fully open architecture. When you access data through it, you are typically accessing the data directly from authoratative sources. These include the British Geological Survey, The Environmemt Agency, Natural England, The British Library, The National Library of Scotland, Ordnance Survey and the Dorset Environmental Records Centre.

GeoFeature...

  • provides easy access to authoritative and other spatial data to the public
  • facilitates the opening of data by regional and local geoconservation bodies
  • provides an open framework of geospatial data services that supports community innovation

To see what data is available on GeoFeature, refer to the Data Catalogue in GeoFeature itself. Click the Data Catalogue tab and expand the Data Set group names.

How to use GeoFeature

Just go to https://geoffrowland.github.io/geofeature3Dnew/. Most operations are obvious, but you can click the Help and FAQ button near the top of this page for specific instructions.

For further information email GeoFeature. Feel free to send us feedback and bug reports.

TerriaJS Software

GeoFeature is based upon Open Source TerriaJS software available as a Github project. The core platform, TerriaJS, was initially developed by Data61 for the NationalMap of Australia, but has subsequently been used for other projects. It has been made available to assist with the development of other spatial data viewing applications.
